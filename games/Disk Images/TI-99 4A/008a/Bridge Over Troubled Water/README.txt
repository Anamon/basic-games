BRIDGE OVER TROUBLED WATER

This is a rendition of Simon and Garfunkel's "Bridge Over Troubled
Water".

This game was found in a collection of old TI disk images.

The original image name and path was "008a.dsk/BRIDGE".

The game is in TI Extended BASIC, and is credited in August 1985 to
Bill Knecht, but no further information is known.
