MORTELLE RANDONNEE

This is a Frogger clone reimagined in terms of a skier trying to make
it to the chalet through snowmobile traffic and ski lifts.

The primary language is French:

Vous devez rejoindre votre chalet en evitant les luges et moto neige,
puis ensautant de telesiege en telepherique.  Tous les 4 chalets voug
gagnez un skieur, mais le jeu accelere.  Vous avez 5 skieurs au
depart.

This game was found in a collection of old TI disk images.

The original image name and path was "0078.dsk/RANDONEE".

The game is in TI Extended BASIC, but no further information is known.
