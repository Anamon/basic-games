SONGS OF TEXAS

This program contains renditions of a collection of "Texas" songs:

  Deep in the Heart of Texas
  The Yellow Rose of Texas
  The Eyes of Texas

This game was found in a collection of old TI disk images.

The original image name and path was "0040.dsk/TEXASONGS".

The game is in TI BASIC and credited to Joe and Carolyn Loghry of
Imboden, AR, but no further information is known.
