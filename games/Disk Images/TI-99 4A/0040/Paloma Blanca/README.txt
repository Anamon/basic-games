PALOMA BLANCA

This is a rendition of "Paloma Blanca", with words and music from an
arrangement by Hans Bouwens in "Music of Today Made Easy for Piano".

This game was found in a collection of old TI disk images.

The original image name and path was "0040.dsk/PALOMA".

The game is in TI BASIC and credited to T. A. Wittbrodt, but no
further information is known.
