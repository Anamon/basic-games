Russian Roulette 

In this game, you are given by the computer a revolver loaded with one
bullet and five empty chambers.  You spin the chamber and pull the
trigger by inputting a "1," or, if you want to quit, input a "2."  You
win if you play ten times and are still alive.

Tom Adametx wrote this program while a student at Curtis Jr. High
School in Sudbury, Massachusetts.
