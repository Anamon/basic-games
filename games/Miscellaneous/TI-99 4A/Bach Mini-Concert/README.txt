BACH MINI-CONCERT

This is a J. S. Bach "Mini-Concert", featuring renditions of "Minuet
in G Minor", "Musette in D", and "Little Prelude in F".

This game was found in a collection of old TI disk images.

The original image name and path was "0040.dsk/BACH3SONGS".

The game is in TI Extended BASIC and credited in 1981 to Leon Tietz
and Curt Garcia, but no further information is known.
