THE 59TH STREET BRIDGE SONG

This is a rendition of the classic Simon & Garfunkel tune.

This game was found in a collection of old TI disk images.

The original image name and path was "0040.dsk/59TH/ST/BR".

The game is in TI BASIC and credited in 1983 to A. DeCicco of Gelford,
NJ, but no further information is known.
