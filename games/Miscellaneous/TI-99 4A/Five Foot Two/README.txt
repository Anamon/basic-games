FIVE FOOT TWO

This is a rendition of "Five Foot Two, Eyes of Blue", coupled with
some mild cartoon nudity.

This game was found in a collection of old TI disk images.

The original image name and path was "0040.dsk/5FOOT2".

The game is in TI Extended BASIC and credited in 1984 to Ediwn McFall
of Aberdeen, WA, but no further information is known.
